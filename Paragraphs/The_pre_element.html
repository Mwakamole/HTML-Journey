The Pre Element Tutorial

For poems or articles with a pre-defined format, the 'p' tag should not be used since the article will be displayed in an undesired format such as in the below example:

My Bonnie lies over the ocean. My Bonnie lies over the sea. My Bonnie lies over the ocean. Oh, bring back my Bonnie to me.

Solution

To solve this problem, the 'pre' tag should be used.
The example below has put the 'pre' tag in use

            My Bonnie lies over the ocean.
          
            My Bonnie lies over the sea.
          
            My Bonnie lies over the ocean.
          
            Oh, bring back my Bonnie to me.
